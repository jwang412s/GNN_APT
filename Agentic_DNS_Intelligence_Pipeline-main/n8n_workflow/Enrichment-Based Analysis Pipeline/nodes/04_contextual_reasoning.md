# Contextual Reasoning

## Purpose
Interprets enriched and correlated intelligence to produce structured analytical insights.

## Description
This stage applies reasoning logic to assess the collected intelligence in context, focusing on coherence, explainability, and analytical consistency.

Reasoning is orchestrated via external reasoning services and structured prompts rather than embedded execution logic. Outputs are derived from prior stages and do not operate on raw indicators.

## Inputs
- Enrichment dataset
- Correlated intelligence signals

## Outputs
- Risk assessment summary
- Structured analytical observations

## Notes
The reasoning stage is designed to support alternative reasoning approaches without impacting upstream data collection.
